Places to Visit in Kottayam

Kumarakom

This stunningly beautiful land located on the Vembanad lake is dotted with plenty of traditional rice boats and canoes!

Poonjar Palace

This is six hundred year old magnificent palace.The palace made almost entirely of wood !

Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls

It's amazing with the roaring sound of water as they cascade down the mountains till they drop down 100ft!

Vagamon

Grass covered hills, deep valleys, beautiful meadows and the green tea plantations made it an ideal tourist spot!

Pathiramanal

Located near Vembanad Lake in Kottayam district Pathiramanal is also called 'Sands of Night'!

Ilaveezhapoonchira

This spot Situated at distance of 55 kms from Kottayam is the most excellent place to witness the sunrise and sunset!
