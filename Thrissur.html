Places to Visit in Thrissur

Athirappilly Waterfalls

Standing 80 ft tall and about 330 ft wide, these falls are the largest in the state of Kerala.!

Shakthan Thampuran Palace

The Shakthan Thampuran Palace was rebuilt in the place of Vadakkekara Palace in the year 1795 by the then King Ramavarma Thampuram of Cochin !

Vazhachal Falls

The milky colour of the water is attracts visitors to come and witness the majesty of the falls situated on the west flowing Chalakudy River!

Vadakkunnathan Temple

Thekkinkadu Maidan, which surrounds the temple from all sides is where the local festival of Thrissur Pooram is organized every year!

Chavakkad Beach

One of the best reasons for people to flock here is the colour of the waters, and the sight of fishermen going about their daily life!

Punnathur Kotta

An Elephant Sanctuary, the Punnathur Kotta was initially a palace belonging to a local ruler. It was then converted to a house of elephants!
