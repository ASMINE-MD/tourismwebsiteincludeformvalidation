Places to Visit in Kasaragod

Bekal Fort

The 300 years old defensive mechanism which is the largest and well-preserved fort makes it one of the best Kasaragod tourist place!

Parappa Wildlife Sanctuary

It is a natural habitat of some underrated fauna like a slow turtle, the prickly porcupine, Malabar hornbill, Slender loris, and the cute wild jungle cat!

Ranipuram Hills

It seeking leisure the greens of shola woods and monsoon forests have all their natural beauty to offer you!

Thaikadappuram Beach

If rare and unexplored fauna catches your attention then you must visit this beach in the monsoon season to watch some olive ridley turtles!

Mallikarjuna Temple

This edifice was built by the Iyer kings and is one of the most revered tourist places in Kasaragod. A temple dedicated to Lord Shiva!

Hosdurg Fort

What makes it so popular is its chain of forts, that came up in Ikkeri dynasty. Visit this monument to unveil the past!
